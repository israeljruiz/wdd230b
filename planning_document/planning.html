Site purpose

The purpose of this site is to inform business owners and those seeking business opportunities on the benefits that the Inqa Chamber of Commerce (ICC) offers. The Inqa Chamber of Commerce seeks to help businesses and business owners by informing them of their rights along with their responsibilities. The ICC provides support and consulting so all those involved in the business cycle can compete better in their markets.
