Target audience

The target audience is all those that wish to do business in the city of Lima, Peru. This includes large, medium and small companies along with entrepreneurs working alone. The Municipality of Lima along with district governments are also an important part of helping business be more competitive.

Personas

Persona 1

Ramon Vada is a mechanic with a small shop employing four other mechanics. He has been living in Lima for 20 years and has relied on his family and own experience to help him grow his business. He has one shop and would like to open two more but needs guidance on the legal implications.

Persona 2

The Municipality of Lima has a new mayor who is looking for local talent to work in newly opened offices. As a part of his campaign, he promised to provide Lima residents with job opportunities so he is turning to the Inqa Chamber of Commerce to connect with people that can work with the Municipality.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

A bakery owner would like to connect with other bakery owners to put together a fair that would generate more business and help position their brands.

Scenario 2

A marketing company would like to receive training for its personnel on business practices that can boost sales.

Scenario 3

A company that exports asparagus to Europe requires information on how to begin exporting blueberries and would like to also connect with international markets.

Scenario 4

A college graduate who just finished their business degree would like to connect with other like-minded individuals that can contribute to their vision.

Scenario 5

Local media would like to do a report on three successful entrepreneurs as a part of their business section.
